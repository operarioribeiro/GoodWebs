# [GoodWebs](https://operarioribeiro.github.com/GoodWebs)

> _"Find your next favorite websites."_

## Open Project:

**GoodWebs** is an open source social network online platform for web lovers where people share, create collections, rate, and review the websites they love with friends and developers so creating and joining groups to receive personalized recommendations based on their profile and preferences. It was inspired by [Good Reads](https://goodreads.com) and [Geocities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GeoCities).
